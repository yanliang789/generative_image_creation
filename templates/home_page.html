Generate Image is an application in which you can generate your own hairstyle based on some reference hairstyle pictures and source pictures to generate new hairstyle pictures.

: There are some default reference hairstyle pictures, or you can upload your own samples under the add_sample URL for reference.

: Through the add_src URL, you can upload a picture as the source picture.

The "style" is the URL of the final hairstyle picture you can use. It usually takes some time to generate.
